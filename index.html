What is SchizoAxes?

SchizoAxes is a quiz, running on a modded base of 8values, that attempts to see what kind of schizoposter you are. You will be presented by a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will becompared to the maximum possible for each value, thus giving you a percentage. Answer honestly!

There are questions in the test.

What are the eight values?

There are five axes - and each has two opposing values assigned to them. They are:

OATLY

Those who score high on the oatly test are more likely to prefer meat alternatives as they trust meat producers more than the makers of vegetarian products.

WHAT WILL YOU NOT EAT

BUGS

Those who refuse to eat bugs are often rejecters of the government, however would be more prone than oatly haters to eat plant-based products.

CORPORATIONS

Those who score higher on the corporations axis tend to distrust corporations more but may be more willing to trust the government.

WHO IS THE ENEMY

GOVERNMENT

Those who distrust the government are more libertarian in nature and may be prone to trust corporations easier than they trust the government.

BETA

Betas tend to be lesser than others, and may follow an Alpha or Sigma, who may encourage them to be more scizo in their ways.

WHAT KIND OF PERSON ARE YOU

SIGMA

Sigmas are dominant but introverted, and are typically more successful. Some sigmas may resort to joining the enemy so that the enemy would be kinder to them, instead of attempting to fight the enemy.

ORGANIZED RELIGION

Those who distrust organized religion believe that large cults are out to get them

WHAT DIVINE ENTITY IS YOUR ENEMY

ANTICHRIST

People who hate the antichrist are often members of organized religions themselves but often deviate from the norm at their churches and houses of worship.

CANNIBALISM

Those who resort to cannibalism often do so for a variety of reasons, but they generally do not believe that there are bugs in human skin.

DO YOU EAT PEOPLE OR DO PEOPLE HAVE BUGS IN THEIR SKIN

BUGS

Those who score higher towards bugs would never eat another person, as that would give them even more bugs.
